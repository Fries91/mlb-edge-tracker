Privacy Policy for MLB Edge Tracker

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Overview

MLB Edge Tracker is a baseball analytics app that displays matchup information, prediction confidence, model health, and historical accuracy tracking.

Personal Data

This app does not ask users to enter personal information such as names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, passwords, or account details.

User Accounts

MLB Edge Tracker does not require user accounts, sign-ins, or profile creation.

Payments

This app does not process payments, subscriptions, purchases, or financial information.

Data Used by the App

The app uses publicly available baseball information such as schedules, game results, team stats, pitcher stats, recent form, matchup factors, and generated prediction history.

Stored App Data

The backend may store prediction history, model-learning data, optimizer rules, factor reports, and sync logs. This data is used to improve the app’s analytics display and model reporting.

Device Data

The app may use normal browser or app storage to cache files so the app loads faster. This cache does not require users to provide personal information.

Third-Party Data

MLB Edge Tracker may use baseball data from public or third-party sports data sources. Those sources may have their own terms and policies.

Advertising

MLB Edge Tracker does not include advertising in this version.

Children

This app is intended as a general sports analytics tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Disclaimer

Predictions are informational only. No result is guaranteed. Baseball outcomes can change due to injuries, lineup changes, weather, pitching changes, and game variance.
